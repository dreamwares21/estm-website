About Escuela Secundaria Tecnica Mexico

History:
ESTM was established in 1983 by the Government of Mexico as a friendly festure in recogniction of Belize's independence. It opened its doors to 68 students and 5 teachers.

Mission:
Escuela Secundaria Técnica México strives to become an educational community integrating learning to produce knowledgeable, highly skilled, and responsible citizens for a productive, cooperative, competitive, progressive, and self-sustainable society.

Vision:
Escuela Secundaria Técnica México will provide quality education, promote knowledge, and develop skills and a positive attitude, in the vocational/technical and academic/business fields through theoretical and practical applications of learning to prepare productive citizens in society.
